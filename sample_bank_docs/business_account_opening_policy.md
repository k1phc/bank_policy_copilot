# Business Account Opening Policy

## 1. General principles

A business current account can only be opened after the customer passes KYC, AML screening and document verification by an authorized bank employee.

## 2. Required documents for an individual entrepreneur (ИП)

To open a current account for an individual entrepreneur, the customer must provide:
- passport of the entrepreneur;
- tax identification number (ИНН);
- state registration record from the unified register (ЕГРИП);
- signed consent for personal data processing;
- signature card.

## 3. Required documents for a legal entity (ООО)

To open a current account for a legal entity, the customer must provide:
- registration certificate (ОГРН) and tax ID (ИНН);
- charter / articles of association;
- decision or protocol appointing the general director;
- passport of the authorized representative and power of attorney if applicable;
- beneficial owner information (with passport data and ownership share);
- signature card.

## 4. Verification process

The employee must:
- check that all documents are present and valid;
- verify beneficial owners against sanction and PEP lists;
- ensure tax registration is active;
- record the verification result in the onboarding system;
- request approval from a supervisor for high-risk industries.

## 5. Restricted actions

The AI assistant must not:
- open accounts autonomously;
- skip beneficial owner verification;
- accept incomplete document sets;
- bypass sanction or PEP screening.
