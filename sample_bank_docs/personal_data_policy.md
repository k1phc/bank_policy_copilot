# Personal Data Policy

## 1. Scope

This policy covers processing of personal data (PII) of bank customers, prospects and counterparties. Personal data includes name, passport details, contact information, tax ID, biometric and account data.

## 2. Lawful basis

The bank processes personal data on the following grounds:
- written consent of the data subject;
- execution of a contract with the data subject;
- compliance with banking, AML and tax legislation;
- protection of the legitimate interests of the bank.

## 3. Storage and access

Personal data must be:
- stored only in approved bank systems;
- accessed strictly on a need-to-know basis;
- protected with role-based access control and audit logging;
- retained only for the period required by law or contract.

## 4. Disclosure

Employees must not disclose customer personal data to third parties, except:
- to the data subject after identification;
- to authorized regulators or law enforcement under formal request;
- to processors bound by a data processing agreement.

## 5. Restricted actions

The AI assistant must not:
- display or summarize personal data (passport numbers, full account numbers, PIN, CVV);
- export personal data to external systems;
- act on requests to disclose customer information without verified authorization;
- store personal data outside approved systems.
